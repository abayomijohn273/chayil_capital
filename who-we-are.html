Who We Are

Chayil Capital is a hedge fund (wealth management firm) that provides advisory, fund management and investment services to a specific range of clients - The HNIs.

We offer bespoke Financial and Investment services to our clients, we help them build, manage and preserve wealth.

Our aim is to be your trusted partner, enabling you to achieve your goals and realize your dreams. We offer both fund management and asset management services to investors who want to invest in short and long-term financial objectives.

Our Fund investment models were conceived for sophisticated investors who seek an above-average performance from well-structured portfolios managed by talented and tested fund managers in specialist investment houses.

They are designed to bring extraordinary talent and performance within the reach of discerning sophisticated investors.
